Meet Ups

This fictional club likes to meet up at its clubhouse twice weekly on Tuesday and Thursday Evenings where we develop our film and share our images for an hour before heading out to shoot more pictures in the anglian countryside.

Memebership

Membership comes with a host of benefits including :

  • Unlimited use of the clubhouse and its film developing rooms
  • Regular camera meet ups; go shooting pictures with a group
  • Access to members area of the website
  • Free entry into weekly photo competitions and upvoted leaderboards

Competitions

Weekly Photo Competitions

  • Members get free entry into each weekly photo competition
  • Cash prizes for the top three photographs each week
  • Non-members can enter and submit their photographs for $5
